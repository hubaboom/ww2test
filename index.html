World War II Study Hub

Revision Guide

Causes of WWII

  • Treaty of Versailles punished Germany after WWI
  • Economic crisis and the Great Depression
  • Rise of dictatorships
  • Failure of the League of Nations
  • Appeasement policies by Britain and France

Major War Fronts

  • Western Front – Germany vs Britain, France, and later USA
  • Eastern Front – Germany vs Soviet Union
  • Pacific Front – Japan vs USA and Allies
  • North African Front – Axis vs Allied forces

Timeline Highlights

  • 1939 – Germany invades Poland
  • 1940 – Battle of Britain
  • 1941 – Pearl Harbor
  • 1942–43 – Stalingrad
  • 1944 – D-Day
  • 1945 – Germany and Japan surrender
